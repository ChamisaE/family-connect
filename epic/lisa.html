Lisa Atkins

Persona

Name: Lisa Atkins

Gender: Female

Age: 47

Marital Status: Married

Profession: Teacher

Location: Lubbock, Texas

Attitude & Behavior

Lisa is busy mom who not only takes care of her own family but also her students at work. She spends lots of time caring after others, volunteering, and wants an easy way to communicate and plan with her family.

Needs

  • Share information with immediate and extended family
  • Share pictures easily with everyone
  • List events on calendars
  • Sign up sheet for potlucks and events

Frustrations

  • Doesn't want it to be too complicated to use.
  • Doesn't like long upload time for pictures.
  • Doesn't like when there aren't certain fun features such as like and dislike buttons.
  • Doesn't like when there is a lag in time for messages sent.

Technology

Goals

  • Needs a way to keep track of her family's lives
  • Wants an easier way to plan activities together
  • keep in touch in a fun, easy way that is more private that social media

User Story

As a user, Lisa would like to plan events and keep up with her family in an easy, executive way.

Use Case

Title: Keep in touch with family throught a more private network thatn social media, wants to keep family informed about events utilizing a calendar feature where she can also post sign in for potluck get togethers.

Description:Lisa wants to add events for everyone to see and sign up for what they will bring to events.

Name of user & their role: Lisa; busy mom who likes to keep in touch with family members.

Preconditions: Must have a Family Connect account and be logged in.

Usage postconditions: Lisa is able to add events to the calendar and see what everyone has signed up to bring to events.

Interaction Flow

  • Lisa types familyconnect.com into her web browser.
  • She is taken to the Family Connect homepage.
  • She logs in with her user id and password.
  • Server completes request and logs her into her account.
  • She goes to her family channel and uploads a picture of her son playing baseball.
  • She then clicks the calendar feature, the server completes the request, and takes her to the calendar.
  • She checks to see what events have been posted by other family members.
  • She sees that her aunt has posted about a potluck next month and sees a sign up sheet associated with the event.
  • She clicks the sign up sheet where the browser redirects her to a new page to sign up for the potluck.
  • She checks off for chips and salsa.
  • She clicks ok and logs out.
