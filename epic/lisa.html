Lisa Atkins

Persona

Name: Lisa Atkins

Gender: Female

Age: 47

Marital Status: Married

Profession: Teacher

Location: Lubbock, Texas

Attitude & Behavior

Lisa is busy mom who not only takes care of her own family but also her students at work. She spends lots of time caring after others, volunteering, and wants an easy way to communicate and plan with her family.

Needs

  • Have an easy way to manage user accounts and share information with immediate and extended family
  • Upload pictures easily so they can be shared with family members
  • List events on calendars that her group family members can see
  • Sign up sheet for potlucks and events

Frustrations

  • Doesn't want it to be too complicated to use.
  • Doesn't like long upload time for pictures.
  • Doesn't like that there aren't certain fun features such as animation.
  • Doesn't like that there's a lag time for messages.

Technology

  • Lisa has a black Samsung Galaxy S9+ with 2.8GHz processor, 2960 x 1440 6.2" Quad HD+ display, 8MP AF front camera, 12MP OIS rear camera, 6GB RAM, 64GB internal memory storage, SIM 1 MicroSD, 3.1 Gen 1 USB Bluetooth v5.0, ANT+, Android O 8.0 OS.
  • MacBook Air 1.6 GHz dual-core 8th-gen Intel Core i5 processor with Turbo Boost, retina display, 8gb 2133MHz LPDDR3 memory, 128GB SSD storage, Intel UHD Graphics 617, touch ID, force touch trackpad, two thunderbold 3 ports.

Goals

Lisa is a teacher who works long hours and needs a way to keep track of her family's lives, an easier way to plan activities together, and keep in touch in a fun, easy way.

User Story

As a user, Lisa would like to plan events with her family in an easy, executive way.

Use Case

Title: Keeping family informed about events utilizing the calendar feature.

Description:Lisa wants to add events that everyone in her group can see and she wants them to be able to sign up for what they will bring to events.

Name of user & their role: Lisa; busy mom who likes to keep in touch with family members.

Preconditions: Lisa must have a Family Connect user id and password and be logged in to the account.

Usage postconditions: Lisa is able to add family members to the website as well as events to the calendar and see what everyone has signed up to bring to events.

Interaction Flow

  • Lisa logs into her Family Connect account and wants to start a new family funsapce.
  • Lisa clicks on the plus sign box to the left of the site that says, "Sign in to a new funspace where she is then redirected to a page that allows for her to sign into a different funspace or to create a new funspace. She clicks on create anew funspace.
  • She is then redirected to create a new funspace and is to enter her email. She enters her email and is then told by the site to check her email for a verification code.
  • Lisa logs into her email and finds the email with a verfication code. She enters the verification code into Family connect and is then redirected to enter her name as she would like the group to see it. She enters "Lisa Atkins".
  • She is then redirected to set a password. Lisa enters a password.
  • After entering in a password and clicking continue, she is redirected to enter a name for the group she is creating. Lisa enters in "Atkin Family" and clicks continue.
  • She is now redirected to enter in a new url for family connect. She enters in "Atkin Family" and clicks create funspace.
  • A window pop up for her to agree to terms of service and click agree. She is then redirected to now enter in the email for those she would like to join the group. Lisa enters in the email of family members and clicks 'send invitations'.
  • Lisa has now created a funspace for "Atkin Family"
  • She logs out.
