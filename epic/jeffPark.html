Jeff Park

About

Name: Jeff Park

Gender: Male

Age: 22

Marital status: Single

Occupation: student

Location: Eugene, Oregon

Attitude

Jeff is an upbeat student originally from Albuquerque. He's very focused on his academics and is highly family oriented, but has found it difficult to stay in touch with his family while he's been attending the University of Oregon.

Needs

  • Ability to directly communicate with extended family members
  • Ability to share his experiences with his family
  • Ability to see the time and place of upcoming family events

Frustrations

  • Jeff doesn't like when he gets push notifications while he's studying
  • Jeff wishes he had the ability to customize the appearance of the app

Technology

Goals

Jeff would like to be able to keep in touch with extended family while he's off at college. He wants to know about family events so that he can try to plan trips home around them. He'd also like to keep his family up with things happening in his life.

User Story

Jeff wants to let his family know that his intramural soccer team won a tournament last weekend.

Use Case

Title: Posting a message about Jeff's intramural soccer victory

Scenario: Jeff's family is full of athletes who like to talk about recent sports events. Jeff's intramural soccer team won a tournament last week and he would like to let his family know about it.

Usage preconditions:

  • Jeff must be part of a family group
  • Jeff must be logged into his Family Connect account
  • Ideally, there will be a specific channel where Jeff can post his message

Usage postconditions:

  • A message detailing Jeff's soccer win will be visible to his family members
  • Jeff's family will be able to add messages to his post and congratulate him

Interaction Flow

  • Jeff opens up his computer and opens up a web browser
  • Jeff enters the URL for "Family Connect" in his navbar
  • Server returns his request, brings him to Family Connect's home page
  • Jeff submits his log in information, server processes his request and brings him to his family's "family group" page
  • Jeff selects his family's #sports channel from the list of channels he belongs to
  • Jeff types up a brief synopsis of last weekend's tournament, adds an image of his team from his computer, and hits enter
  • Jeff's message and image are posted and are now visible to all the family members who belong to the #sports channel
